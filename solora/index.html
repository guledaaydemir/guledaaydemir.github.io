The method

What is the Big 3 Method?

Every day, you choose just three Most Important Tasks. Not thirty. Three. The Big 3 is a focus practice: when everything is a priority, nothing is — so you commit to the three that genuinely matter and let momentum do the rest.

  • Three is achievable. Even a chaotic day usually has room for three wins, so you almost always end the day ahead.
  • Choosing is the work. Deciding what matters most is harder — and more valuable — than doing busywork.
  • Roles keep it balanced. Solora nudges your Big 3 across different roles so one part of life doesn't quietly swallow the rest.
  • Nothing is lost. Unfinished focus carries forward to tomorrow instead of vanishing into a backlog.

Questions

Good to know.

What is the Big 3 method?
The Big 3 is a daily focus method where you pick the three Most Important Tasks for the day. Instead of drowning in a long list, you commit to finishing three meaningful things — so progress is guaranteed even on busy days.
How is Solora different from a normal to-do list?
A normal to-do list piles up tasks until you feel buried. Solora starts from your roles — like parent, founder, student or athlete — and a daily Big 3, so your day reflects who you are and what truly matters, not just everything you could do.
Is Solora free?
Yes. Solora is free to download and use, with an optional Premium upgrade that unlocks unlimited roles, tasks, habits and projects, plus unlimited AI, deeper analytics and premium themes.
Does Solora use AI, and is my data private?
Solora's assistant runs on Apple's on-device intelligence. Your tasks, habits and AI conversations stay on your iPhone — Solora has no server that collects your content.
What devices and languages does Solora support?
Solora is built for iPhone on iOS 26 and is fully available in English and Turkish, with instant in-app language switching.
