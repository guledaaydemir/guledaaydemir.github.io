SOLO.SHOTS Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Your privacy is the foundation of SOLO.SHOTS. This app is built on a simple principle: your photos and progress data live on your device and in your personal iCloud account — never on our servers. This Policy explains what (very little) data we interact with and how we handle it.

01 — Who We Are

SOLO.SHOTS ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is developed and operated by Gül Eda Aydemir, an independent iOS developer. For any privacy inquiries, contact us at the address listed at the bottom of this document.

02 — Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we never do:

  • We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos on any developer-controlled server.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, address) during normal app use.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not build profiles of you or your behavior beyond what is necessary for core app functionality.

03 — Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All core app data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's native frameworks (SwiftData / Core Data / file system):

  • Photos you capture via the App, including daily progress images.
  • Project metadata — project names, creation dates, notes, and settings.
  • App preferences — theme selection, notification preferences, and onboarding state.
  • Streak and milestone data — calculated and stored entirely on-device.

If you enable iCloud Backup in your device settings, this data may be included in your personal iCloud backup. This backup is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and is accessible only to you.

04 — Third-Party Services

Google AdMob Free Tier Only

The free version of the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data including device identifiers, IP address, and interaction data to serve personalized or contextual ads, subject to your device's Ad Tracking settings. You can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iOS device.

AdMob's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. Upgrading to SOLO.SHOTS Premium completely removes all advertisements.

Apple StoreKit / App Store Premium Subscription

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple's App Store via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple handles all billing, receipt validation, and subscription management. Apple's data practices are governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

Apple Analytics

If you have opted in to share diagnostic and usage data with Apple (Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements → Share iPhone Analytics), Apple may share anonymized, aggregated analytics data with us via App Store Connect. This data does not include personally identifiable information and is used solely to identify crashes and improve performance.

05 — Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library to enable its core functionality. These permissions are:

  • Camera: Required to capture daily progress photos within the App.
  • Photo Library: Required (on supported premium tiers) to import existing photos into a project.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings → SOLO.SHOTS. Photos captured or imported are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

06 — Notifications

The App may request permission to send you local push notifications (e.g., daily reminders to capture your progress photo). These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device. We do not use a push notification server and do not transmit your device push token to any third-party service.

07 — Children's Privacy

SOLO.SHOTS is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

08 — Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under applicable data protection law (including GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and others). Since we do not maintain a server-side database of user data, most rights (access, deletion, portability) are exercised directly on your device:

  • Delete your data: Uninstalling the App from your device removes all locally stored app data.
  • iCloud data: Manage or delete iCloud backups via Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.
  • Ad preferences: Adjust via Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

For any privacy request that cannot be resolved on-device, contact us at the address below.

09 — Data Security

Because your data is stored on your own device, its security is protected by iOS's built-in security features, including hardware-level encryption and Face ID / Touch ID. We encourage you to keep your device software up to date and use a strong device passcode.

10 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice or an App Store update note. Continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
