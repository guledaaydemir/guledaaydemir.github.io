Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Solo.Swipe ("we", "us", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Solo.Swipe, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

Solo.Swipe does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Photos or videos from your library
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Any form of tracking data

We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking frameworks.

3. Photo Library Access

Solo.Swipe requires access to your photo library solely to display photos for review within the app. All photo processing occurs entirely on your device using Apple's PhotoKit framework. Photos are never uploaded, copied, transmitted, or stored outside of Apple's native Photos framework. When you delete photos through Solo.Swipe, they are moved to Apple's "Recently Deleted" album where you can recover them within 30 days.

4. Local Storage

The only data stored locally on your device includes:

  • App preferences (appearance, language, notification settings)
  • Session statistics (number of photos reviewed, deleted, kept)
  • Album identifiers for albums created through the app
  • Streak data (daily usage count for gamification)

All data is stored using Apple's UserDefaults and remains exclusively on your device. No data is synced to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

Solo.Swipe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services. The app uses only Apple's native frameworks (PhotoKit, StoreKit, UserNotifications, WidgetKit). No personal data, photos, or photo metadata is shared with any third party.

6. Notifications

Solo.Swipe may send local push notifications to remind you of daily cleaning sessions and streak progress. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings or your device's notification settings.

7. Home Screen Widget

Solo.Swipe offers an optional home screen widget that displays your streak count and a daily photo reminder. Widget data is shared between the main app and the widget extension through a local App Group container on your device. No data leaves your device. The widget accesses a single photo from your library to display as a thumbnail; this photo is never uploaded or stored externally.

8. Children's Privacy

Solo.Swipe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such data.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Since Solo.Swipe does not collect or transmit any data to external servers, there is no personal data stored remotely to delete. All locally stored preferences and statistics are removed when you uninstall the app. You can also manually reset your statistics and review history from the app's Settings > Data section at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Solo.Swipe after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at soloswipeapp@gmail.com or on Instagram @solo.stasera.

Your privacy is our priority. Solo.Swipe was built with a simple principle: your photos are yours, and they should stay that way.
