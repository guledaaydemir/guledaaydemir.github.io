Privacy Policy

Effective: April 11, 2026

📋 Overview

ACHii ("the App") is a digital strategy board game developed by Gül Eda Aydemir. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. We are committed to keeping your data safe and being transparent about our practices.

🎮 Game Center Data

ACHii uses Apple Game Center for authentication, leaderboards, and multiplayer matchmaking. When you sign in via Game Center, we access:

  • Your Game Center Player ID (a pseudonymous identifier)
  • Your Game Center display name

This data is used solely to identify you within the game, track your Elo rating and match history, and enable online multiplayer. We do not access your Apple ID, real name, or email through Game Center.

📢 Advertising

ACHii uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements to non-premium users. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when consented via App Tracking Transparency)
  • General device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

ACHii respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You will be prompted to allow or deny tracking. If denied, only contextual (non-personalised) ads are served. Premium users see no ads and no ad-related data is collected for them.

For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

☁️ Cloud Sync & Local Storage

ACHii uses Apple CloudKit to sync your profile data (username, Elo ratings, achievements, match history) across your devices signed into the same iCloud account. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible by other users or by us.

Locally, ACHii uses SwiftData (on-device database) and UserDefaults to store:

  • Game preferences (sound, theme, language settings)
  • Economy state (coins, lives, streak progress)
  • Match history and Elo ratings
  • Feature flags and daily ad counters

All local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

🔒 Data We Do Not Collect

ACHii does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your real name, email address, or phone number
  • Contacts, photos, or location data
  • Health, financial, or biometric information
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

👶 Children's Privacy

ACHii does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). The App uses Game Center for authentication, which is managed by a parent or guardian's Apple ID settings. If we learn that data has been inadvertently collected from a child, we will delete it promptly.

🔄 Data Retention & Deletion

Your game data persists as long as you use the App. You can delete all local data by uninstalling ACHii. iCloud-synced data can be removed via Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage. Game Center data is managed through Apple's Game Center settings.

🌍 Third-Party Services

ACHii integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Game Center — Authentication & multiplayer
  • Apple CloudKit — Cross-device data sync
  • Google AdMob — Advertising (non-premium users)
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchases

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
